# Style Profiles

Use one profile at a time. These are distilled patterns, not source transcripts.

## `huo-nam-romantic-object`

Best for: worshipping a mundane object, product, food, hobby, app, or lifestyle choice with lonely masculine melodrama.

Structure: narrator experiences X, realizes ordinary partner/friends cannot understand, dismisses mainstream alternatives, describes X as having history/trials/fire, envies X for having fitting companions, sits alone in a related location, sees a stranger who also understands X, shares silent recognition, leaves while holding back tears, ends with a tragic identity.

Slot map: sacred object; rejected alternatives; process/trial that proves worth; companion items; lonely location; witness figure; token drink/object; final identity.

Rewrite notes: Use sincere overstatement and slow emotional beats. The joke is that X is too small for the narrator's epic seriousness. Keep direct source wording minimal.

## `ga-je-rumor-escalation`

Best for: fake-serious complaint, workplace injustice, customer-service drama, brand/celebrity grievance, or rumor escalation.

Structure: declare seriousness/anger, introduce close relative or proxy with stable respectable job/status, describe normal life and future plans, introduce target, add unreasonable demand, proxy tries to handle politely, complaint/warning follows, target returns with accomplice, situation escalates, official consequences arrive, final loss lands bluntly.

Slot map: narrator; victim/proxy; workplace/platform; target; unreasonable demand; manager/system rule; warning letters or penalties; final consequence.

Rewrite notes: Avoid presenting real allegations as fact. For real people or companies, make the scenario clearly fictional, generalized, or opinion/parody.

## `forum-definition`

Best for: defining a forum, fandom, app, team, workplace, school, or social circle with cynical affection.

Structure: someone asks what X is; narrator gives a blunt answer; lists contradictory rules and social rituals; says not to take it seriously; ends with the listener memorizing the lesson or being doomed by it.

Slot map: asker; community; apparent purpose; real behavior; hypocrisy; survival advice; final reaction.

Rewrite notes: Keep it short and punchy. This profile works well as a paragraph or as dialogue.

## `public-scene-punchline`

Best for: short anecdote in public where a private truth is accidentally exposed.

Structure: narrator is in a public vehicle/place late or under pressure; nearby people argue; repeated insults or accusations build tension; one line flips the insult back and reveals too much; whole scene freezes; narrator closes with a fake moral lesson.

Slot map: public place; arguing parties; repeated accusation; comeback line; audience reaction; closing lesson.

Rewrite notes: Default to non-explicit embarrassment. Do not include sexual insults unless the user asks for crude style.

## `mc-hardship-romance`

Best for: praising a person, tool, habit, or principle that accepts humble reality rather than glamorous expectations.

Structure: open with a forum setup; friends list idealized standards; narrator asks the practical test; compare expensive/showy alternatives; show past disappointment; discover someone/something that accepts the humble option; inspect for hidden cost; realize it is genuine; define real value; end with a short decisive verdict.

Slot map: idealized standards; practical test; expensive alternatives; humble choice; past disappointment; genuine gesture; value definition; final verdict.

Rewrite notes: Use class anxiety, wallet reality, and sincere comic gratitude. Good endings are short, e.g. "值得留低", "跟得過", "買得過".

## `food-1999-counter`

Best for: absurd customer-service confusion, bad UX, contradictory menu/checkout logic, or bureaucracy.

Structure: narrator orders X at stated price; staff/system asks about add-on; narrator chooses or refuses; staff gives impossible statement; narrator becomes confused; price changes anyway; final bizarre question/comment appears; narrator ends with a short outburst.

Exact beat map for 白汁雞皇飯 original:

1. Narrator says they went to buy/order a shortened item name, then clarifies the item has an odd component relationship and gives the base price.
2. Staff asks whether to add a drink/add-on.
3. Narrator answers with obvious confidence and names the add-on.
4. Staff replies with an impossible category statement, creating the 1999 logic break.
5. Narrator says they do not understand what the staff is saying.
6. Narrator withdraws the add-on.
7. Staff gives a higher total anyway, with `:o)` style deadpan.
8. Narrator refuses again.
9. Staff suddenly asks an unrelated bizarre question.
10. Narrator ends with a crude angry outburst plus repeated `:o)` and `on99:o)` rhythm.

Slot map: order/action; base price; add-on; impossible reply; unexpected total; nonsense question; final reaction.

Rewrite notes: Keep grammar clipped and slightly broken. This profile should usually be short.

## `premium-cheap-object`

Best for: treating cheap consumer goods, low-end tools, instant food, or everyday routines as elite taste.

Structure: set a refined private scene; introduce ordinary X with vintage/rarity language; describe preparation with luxurious sensory detail; taste/use X slowly; connect its ordinary quality to life philosophy; end by naming the year/batch/version as profound.

Slot map: refined setting; cheap object; fake vintage/version; vessel/tool; sensory detail; philosophical turn; closing label.

Rewrite notes: Contrast expensive diction with cheap reality. Do not overdo length; one lush paragraph is enough.

## `tech-tribal-wisdom`

Best for: product/platform tribalism, smug early-adopter identity, or "my setup proves I understand life" arguments.

Structure: narrator sees rival users everywhere and smiles; says using rival product is punishment enough; lists flaws rival users do not notice; lists multitasking/superior features of chosen product; imagines an extreme life scenario where chosen product wins; ends with slogan-like "X,掌握一切,生活智慧" transformed for the topic.

Slot map: rival tribe; chosen product; visible flaw; hidden feature; extreme scenario; slogan.

Rewrite notes: Avoid misogynistic source beats by default. Replace sexual conquest with user-relevant scenarios such as work, travel, study, gaming, or survival.

## `mtr-apology-slogan`

Best for: public-service campaigns, infrastructure disruption, companies apologizing while inconveniencing users, or bland corporate empathy.

Structure: corporate voice says sorry for inconvenience; reframes pain as progress; asks public to tolerate disruption; slogan mutates into sarcastic variants; paragraph exposes the gap between caring language and lived frustration.

Slot map: institution; inconvenience; official reason; sacrifice requested; slogan; sarcastic slogan variant; affected public.

Rewrite notes: This is mild public affairs. Keep it about services, companies, or bureaucracy unless user explicitly requests politics.

## `jackie-ad-testimonial`

Best for: ad parody, product endorsement, influencer review, suspicious marketing claims, or "I was skeptical at first" copy.

Structure: narrator says they first refused; explains they cannot endorse immediately; insists they had to try first; rejects fake effects; describes trial period; says it felt comfortable/effective; says they use it every day and shared it with their team/family; tells director/audience not to add effects; ends by promising the audience will see the same result.

Slot map: product/service; first refusal; reason for skepticism; test period; result; people narrator shared with; no-fake-effects claim; final assurance.

Rewrite notes: Use repeated pauses and reassurance. Works well for mock ads and brand/product opinions.

## `see-gum-dik-setup`

Best for: opening any fake-serious forum story, confession, rant, or sweet/absurd anecdote.

Structure: begin with `是咁的`; establish narrator and ordinary situation; reveal why the matter needs explaining; proceed into another profile if needed.

Slot map: opener; narrator role; setting; incident; reason for post.

Rewrite notes: This is usually a modifier, not a full template. Pair with another profile when the user wants a complete paragraph.

## `cold-breath-concern`

Best for: mock-formal alarm, moral panic, disappointed authority voice, or exaggerated concern about a trivial discovery.

Structure: "when I learned X, I felt shocked/chilled"; narrator worries for group Y; target is framed with scare quotes or formal title; final sentence says the situation reflects a deeper failure.

Slot map: discovery; group being worried for; target role/title; moral judgment; deeper implication.

Rewrite notes: Keep it mild unless the user wants public-affairs parody. This profile is useful as a sentence embedded inside another paragraph.

## `baak-seoi-argument-correction`

Aliases: `白水`, `白水argument`, `白水結構`.

Best for: short argumentative correction where the user wants the 白水 shape applied to a new topic. Use when prompts say `潮文：白水`, `reference 白水`, or `參考 白水`.

Length: keep close to the original. Default to 4 compact numbered moves or 4 short paragraphs, roughly 180-280 Chinese characters total. Do not expand into a long essay unless the user asks for a longer version.

Structure:

1. Correct the other side's framing of the requested topic: besides the supposed benefit/attention/value they mention, the topic also carries ignored costs, pressures, distortions, or harms; some of those harms are exactly what the other side is calling the benefit.
2. Explain that a related "X cannot be Y" belief is a structural stereotype/norm created by the relevant system around the topic; blame that structure, not the wrong target.
3. Question where the topic's supposed advantage/attention/value comes from: why does it exist, who produces it, and if it is unfair, what structure made it that way?
4. End with the landing: all three problems come from the same structure. Acknowledge the other side's difficulty, but insist they must understand what constitutes/creates the difficulty instead of blaming the wrong target.

Slot map: requested topic; so-called benefit/value/attention; ignored cost or pressure; structural norm; wrong target being blamed; true structure creating the problem; final "what constitutes it" landing.

Rewrite notes: The requested `topic:` is the main content. The original 白水 subjects (`女性`, `男性`, `父權社會`) are source slots to map, not literal subjects to preserve, unless the user explicitly asks for the gender/patriarchy topic. For `topic：十八件麥樂雞`, center the piece on `十八件麥樂雞` and map roles to `所謂抵食/滿足`, `食唔晒/被望住/罪惡感`, `一個人唔可以食咁多`, and `快餐消費文化/套餐制度/社交凝視/資本主義`. Prefer explicit `1、2、3` when structure fidelity matters.
